Dian Sastrowardoyo | Globe Asia's 99 Most Inspiring Women

Dian Sastrowardoyo became one of the most recognizable faces in the Indonesian film industry after playing teen heroine Cinta in 2002's classic “Ada Apa Dengan Cinta?” Stardom did not stop Dian, the grand daughter of great Indonesian poet and critic Subagio Sastrowardoyo, from continuing her education. She studied law at the prestigious University of Indonesia before eventually graduating with a bachelor’s degree in philosophy in 2007. In 2014 she graduated cum laude from a Masters in Management program at the same university. Since then, Dian has dabbled in health-food business by establishing 3skinnyminnies, catering service MAM, a health-food restaurant and most recently GreenWok, a range of healthy Indonesian food meal boxes.
